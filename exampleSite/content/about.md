This cookbook is a personal collection of recipes gathered over many years of cooking for family and friends. The focus is on seasonal, unfussy food — dishes that reward patience and good ingredients over complexity.

## How this started

I started keeping notes on recipes after realising I was making the same dishes from memory and never quite the same way twice. Writing them down forced precision. Over time the notes became recipes, and the recipes became this site.

Most of the food here draws from Mediterranean, East Asian, and South American traditions — not because I've mastered any of them, but because they've taught me the most about cooking with confidence and economy.

## On the recipes

Every recipe here has been cooked at home, usually multiple times. Quantities are tested, timings are honest, and substitutions are noted where I know them to work. Where a recipe is adapted from a specific source I've credited it.

Recipes are organised by course. Tags indicate cuisine origin and dietary notes. Use the filter on the recipes page to narrow things down.
